Neurovation üöÄ
Mandatory ¬∑ All Members
Neurovation is GMS's annual health innovation competition ‚Äî a multi-day event where teams pitch, build, and present solutions at the intersection of neuroscience, health tech, and social good. Think of it as a startup pitch competition meets science expo, judged by real industry professionals, faculty, and mentors from our partner ecosystem.
Two independent competition tracks run simultaneously, each open beyond just Venture Lab members:
üéì Undergraduate Track
Open to all Rutgers undergrads. Teams form, develop a health or biotech innovation, and pitch to a panel of judges from industry, academia, and VC. Projects can be research-based, product-based, or social impact-driven. Winners receive recognition + potential funding access.
üè´ High School Track
GMS-invited high school students compete in their own division ‚Äî presenting innovations in health and science to mentors and judges. Designed to expose the next generation to research, entrepreneurship, and the healthcare space early.
As a GMS member ‚Äî each committee is responsible for running the event component they're assigned. But you're free to also participate in or attend any track or competition that your committee isn't responsible for running. Neurovation is a full org event; you don't need to be in Venture Lab to compete or attend.
